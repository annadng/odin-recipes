Budae Jjigae

Description

Loaded with kimchi, spam, sausages, ramen noodles and much more! This is the best and most popular Korean hot pot dish.

Ingredients

Main
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • 200g spam
  • 4 cocktail frankfurt sausages
  • 250g tofu
  • 200g enoki mushrooms
  • 200g king oyster mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup aged kimchi
  • 110g instant ramen noodles
  • 50g korean rice cakes
  • 30g green onion
  • 1-2 slices cheese
Sauce
  • 2 tbsp korean chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp rice wine
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp korean chilli paste
  • Few sprinkles ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Assemble the main ingredients (except for instant ramen noodles, rice cakes, green onion and cheese) in a shallow pot.
  2. Mix all sauce ingredients in a small bowl. Add the sauce in the middle of the pot
  3. Pour the stock in the corner of the pot. Close the lid and boil it on medium high heat until the stock starts to boil (about 8 mins).
  4. Add the remaining ingredients – instant ramen noodles, rice cakes, green onion and cheese on top of the pot and boil uncovered until the noodles are cooked (about 2 to 3 mins).
  5. Reduce the heat to low (if you’re cooking on a portable burner and sharing the food at the dinning table).
  6. Start dishing out soup, protein and vegetables onto your own soup bowl. Serve with steamed rice (& with other Korean side dishes).
